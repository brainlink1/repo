What is BrainLink?

An innovation competition that creates a bridge between universities and companies, encouraging creativity, collaboration, and applied learning.

BrainLink is an innovation competition bringing together business and engineering students to solve real problems proposed by partner companies. It encourages creativity, collaboration, and applied learning through teamwork and mentorship.

Students apply their knowledge while companies gain fresh perspectives and access to future talent. This competition serves as a practical platform for students to demonstrate their skills and for companies to identify potential future employees.

The competition targets university students from both business and engineering disciplines, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration that mirrors real-world business challenges.

Collaboration

Teams of business and engineering students work together to develop comprehensive solutions.

Innovation

Encouraging creative problem-solving and out-of-the-box thinking for real business challenges.

Excellence

Striving for high-quality solutions through research, analysis, and professional presentation.

Real Impact

Solutions with practical applications that can make a tangible difference for partner companies.

Competition Phases

The competition unfolds in two exciting phases, each designed to challenge participants and showcase their innovative solutions.

01

Semifinals

Initial Presentations

Teams present their initial solutions to a panel of judges. The best teams from this round will advance to the finals. This phase focuses on the core concept, feasibility, and potential impact of the proposed solutions.

02

Finals

Expert Panel Judging

Finalists present refined solutions to an expert panel including representatives from Henkel Algeria. This phase evaluates implementation strategy, business viability, and presentation quality to determine the winning team.

Our Partner

BrainLink is proud to partner with industry leaders who provide real-world challenges and mentorship.

Henkel Algeria

Henkel operates worldwide with leading brands and technologies in three business areas: Laundry & Home Care, Beauty Care, and Adhesive Technologies. Founded in 1876, Henkel looks back on more than 140 years of success.

Through this partnership, Henkel Algeria provides real business challenges for students to solve, offering valuable industry insights and access to experienced mentors. This collaboration creates a direct pipeline between academic excellence and industry needs.

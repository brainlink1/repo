What is BrainLink?

An innovation competition that creates a bridge between universities and companies, encouraging creativity, collaboration, and applied learning.

BrainLink is an innovation competition bringing together business and engineering students to solve real problems proposed by partner companies. It encourages creativity, collaboration, and applied learning through teamwork and mentorship.

Students apply their knowledge while companies gain fresh perspectives and access to future talent. This competition serves as a practical platform for students to demonstrate their skills and for companies to identify potential future employees.

The competition targets university students from both business and engineering disciplines, fostering interdisciplinary collaboration that mirrors real-world business challenges.

Collaboration

Teams of business and engineering students work together to develop comprehensive solutions.

Innovation

Encouraging creative problem-solving and out-of-the-box thinking for real business challenges.
